About ReCoDa

Article

The related article Relicensing Combined Datasets by Adrian Wilke, Arwa Bannoura and Axel-Cyrille Ngonga Ngomo was accepted at the Resource Track of the 15th IEEE International Conference on Semantic Computing (ICSC2021).

Code and Data

The Code and Data are availabe at https://github.com/dice-group/ReCoDa. The generated knowledge bases are based on cc.licenserdf (GitHub), the Creative Commons License Compatibility Chart (Wiki) and the European Data Portal Licence Compatibility Matrix (XLSX file).
